Sinigang na Baboy

⏱️ 40 minutes

Select Number of Servings:

Ingredients

  • • 300600900 g Pork belly
  • • 123 pack Sinigang mix
  • • 246 pcs Tomatoes
  • • 123 pc Onion
  • • 100200300 g Kangkong
  • • 100200300 g Radish
  • • 123 pc Eggplant
  • • 80016002400 ml Water
  • • 246 tbsp Fish sauce

Instructions

  1. 1Boil pork in water until tender, about 20 minutes
  2. 2Add tomatoes and onions, cook until soft
  3. 3Add radish and eggplant, simmer for 5 minutes
  4. 4Stir in sinigang mix until fully dissolved
  5. 5Season with fish sauce to taste
  6. 6Add kangkong leaves last and cook for 1-2 minutes
  7. 7Turn off heat once kangkong wilts
  8. 8Serve hot with rice
